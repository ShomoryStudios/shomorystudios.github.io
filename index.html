Collaboration Criteria

This section gives an overview of the criteria we are expecting every potential collaborator to meet.

  • Reference Past Work: Collaborators will be asked to provide references to past projects of their work in order to provide a rough outline of their skill and style.

  • Commitment to Completion: Collaborators should be able to finish projects in a timely manner and demonstrate a commitment to follow through and complete the project.

  • Handling of Feedback: Collaborators should be open to feedback and be willing to make desired changes to ensure the project's consistency with its purpose.

  • Availability: While we don't ask for constant availability, we do expect that collaborators can be reached and respond within reasonable timeframes.

  • No Studio Access: Due to security reasons, we currently do not allow studio access and kindly ask every collaborator to send their projects in their respective file format.

Compensation Models

This section gives an overview of the various types of collaborations we are currently open to.

  • One-Time Payment: A single, non-recurring payment to the collaborator that depends on the size of the project. Usually, the collaborator will be a sum up front and receive the remaining compensation after satisfactory completion of the project.
    • Bonus after Success: Should the game be deemed successful enough by our assessment, an additional bonus will be rewarded.


We currently do not offer any form of royalty payouts at the moment, as we are a very new game studio and have yet to fully establish the foundation for our endeavors. As we progress, we will consider opportunities for this type of compensation in the future.
